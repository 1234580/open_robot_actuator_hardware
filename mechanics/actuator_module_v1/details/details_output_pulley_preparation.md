Details Output Pulley Preparation
=======================

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_1.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Output pulley - oversized diameter.*

Description
-----------

The dimensions of 3d printed parts can vary quite a bit depending on the machine, the printing technology and the material.  
That's why we print the diameters that has to fit the bearings a bit oversize and machine the part to the right diameter on a lathe.

## Pulley for Hip AA Actuator module

<img src="../images/hip_aa_output_pulley.png" width="400"> <br>*Hip AA Output Pulley - don't install helicoils*

Instructions
-------------

<img src="../images/collet_1.jpg" width="400"> <br>*A 25mm collet helps with clamping the output pulley.*  

<img src="../images/lathe_1.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Place the pulley on the lathe and clamp gently on one of the flanges.*  

<img src="../images/lathe_3.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Use a cutting tool with a sharp edge.*

<img src="../images/lathe_2.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Reduce the flange diameter in small steps and check the fit with the output bearing.*  

<img src="../images/lathe_4.jpg" width="400"> <br>*There should be a light press-fit between the bearing and the pulley.<br> You should be able to install and remove the bearing by hand with moderate force.<br> When the diameter is correct - turn the pulley around and repeat the same steps for the opposite side.*  

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_3.jpg" width="600"> <br>*Output Pulley and M3 Helicoil Tools (Helicoil tap, insertion tool and tang break-off tool)*  

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_2.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Output Pulley AT3 T30 and M3 Helicoil inserts*  

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_4.jpg" width="400"> <br>*The holes should have a diameter of about 3mm.<br> Increase the hole diamter with a 3mm drill if the holes are too tight.*

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_5.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Tap both holes on the side with the flange using the Helicoil M3 tap.*

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_6.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Place a M3 6mm Helicoil threaded insert on the insertion tool.*

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_7.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Insert the Helicoils with the insertion tool.<br> Make sure that the insert is about half a turn below the outer surface*

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_8.jpg" width="400"> <br>*Remove both tangs using the tang break-off tool.<br> Make sure that the tangs don't stay inside of the part.*

<img src="../images/output_pulley_preparation_9.jpg" width="400"> <br>*The output pulley is now ready for installation.*
